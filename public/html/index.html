CATARINA MOURO, SOPRANO

Catarina is a portuguese Soprano born in 1996. She studied at the Escola de Musica do Conservatório Nacional in Lisbon.

Winner of the second place in Concurso Internacional de Música da Cidade de Almada, category Canto II (2018).

Currently she is studying with maestro and pianist Dale Fundling.
